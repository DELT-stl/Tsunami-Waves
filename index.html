Welcome to the Tsunami Waves Foundation website. This organization was founded by Carlos Martinez in 2015 with the goal of helping children in need. This cause is very close to his heart as he was once one of those children. With Tsunami Waves we are hopeful to assist in this effort by providing items such as clothing, school supplies, and his favorite… baseball equipment.
